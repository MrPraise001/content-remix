Frequently Asked Questions

How does Content Remix maintain my brand voice?

Our advanced AI analyzes your writing style, tone, and brand voice from your original content. It then applies these characteristics while optimizing for each platform, ensuring consistency across all your content.

What types of content can I transform?

Content Remix supports blogs, articles, video transcripts, podcast transcripts, and long-form social media posts. Our AI can transform these into tweets, LinkedIn posts, Instagram captions, TikTok scripts, and more.

How does the credit system work?

One credit equals one content transformation. For example, turning one blog post into a tweet thread counts as one credit. The Starter plan includes 500 credits, while the Pro plan offers unlimited transformations.
